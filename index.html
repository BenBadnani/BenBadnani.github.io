I recently earned my Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence from Boston University,
where I also completed my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

My interests include big data algorithms, machine learning, and in general, all that

under the guidance of my advisor, Krzysztof Onak.

As an undergraduate, I had the opportunity to engage in various areas of computer science.
I worked as an NLP/Deep Learning engineer in collaboration with BU Spark,
participated in research on black box adversarial attacks at BU, and enjoyed working as
a teaching assistant for a course on sublinear space algorithms.

As a personal sidenote, no course captivated me more than Real Analysis, and I am determined
to eventually complete "Papa Rudin", no matter how long it takes.
